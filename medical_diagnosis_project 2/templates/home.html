Medical Diagnosis System

Welcome to the Medical Diagnosis System, a powerful tool designed to assist healthcare professionals and patients in determining possible medical conditions based on symptoms. Our goal is to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of medical diagnoses by guiding users through a structured process of symptom analysis and decision-making.
